About

Russel Kelly is a media producer whose work spans video production, training content systems, and digital media. His work focuses on translating complex information into clear, engaging visual media.

With experience spanning instructional design, broadcast production, and commercial media, Russel has developed onboarding systems, produced community television content, and created promotional and instructional content for organizations.

He approaches projects with a focus on structure, clarity, and storytelling so that information is not only visually compelling but genuinely useful for the audiences it serves. He also produces social media content, bringing a strong instinct for identifying moments that can be transformed into engaging short-form content.
